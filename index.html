About

Prepare to be electrified by the high-voltage thrill of Minnal Murali, a 2021 Indian Malayalam-language superhero blockbuster directed by Basil Joseph and produced by Sophia Paul. This action-packed adventure introduces us to Jaison, a young tailor who undergoes a jaw-dropping transformation after a lightning strike, emerging as a sensational superhero. With Tovino Thomas and Guru Somasundaram at the helm, the movie promises an exhilarating experience as Jaison takes on the responsibility of safeguarding his hometown from a nefarious antagonist.
